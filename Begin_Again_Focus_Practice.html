Focus

Begin Again

Focus is built by returning.

Your attention will wander. You may get distracted, pulled into thoughts, irritated with yourself, or emotionally hooked.

The skill is not perfect focus. The skill is noticing you left and coming back without turning it into a shame spiral.

Begin Again means: every time you notice you are distracted, that moment becomes the practice.

Pick one focus target.

Choose one simple place to return your attention. Keep it small and concrete.

Or write your own

Choose a simple label.

When your mind wanders, name it lightly. No scolding. No speech. Just a label, then return.

Your return phrase will appear here.

Set a short practice.

You are not trying to win at focus. You are practicing the return.
