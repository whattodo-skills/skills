Inner Critic Practice

The inner critic is a thought pattern, not the truth.

It often shows up as harsh judgments, self-blame, shame, painful assumptions, or a story your mind tells when emotions are loud.

This practice helps you notice the critic, separate facts from judgments, validate the feeling underneath, and choose a next action that actually helps.

The aim: stop treating the harsh thought as fact. Notice it, name it, check it, and move toward what works.
