Ride the Wave

Stay with a strong pull without acting right away.

A strong pull can be noticed without being obeyed. The goal is to observe what changes or remains the same and create time before deciding what to do.

Before you begin

Ride the Wave is a self-guided skill for everyday urges and difficult moments. Use it only when you are safe enough to pause and practice.

Return to Skills

An urge is a wave, not a command

The wave is a metaphor—not a promise about timing. Your pull may ease, strengthen, move, or stay the same. You are practicing noticing it without reacting automatically.

Practice: Check what you notice, pause for a chosen amount of time, recheck, then choose what to do next.
