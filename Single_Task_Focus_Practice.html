Single-Task Focus Practice

Your attention is one of the ways you show love.

It is easy to be physically home but mentally somewhere else — scrolling, checking work, replaying a conversation, or half-listening while someone you care about is talking.

This practice helps you choose one meaningful moment, notice what pulls your focus away, and gently return your attention without shaming yourself.

The skill: Choose one thing. Notice the pull. Return to what matters. Repeat.
