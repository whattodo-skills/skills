Set Clear Intentions For Your Focus

Decide exactly when, where, and how focused action will begin.

Action can begin before motivation.

You may know what needs to be done and still feel frozen. A clear intention gives your focus a starting cue, a tiny action, and a short time limit.

The intention formula: When [cue] happens, I will [tiny first step] for [duration].

The goal is not to finish everything. The goal is to begin intentionally and learn what helps you return to focused action.
