Set Clear Intentions For Your Focus

Do not wait until you feel motivated.

You may know what needs to be done and still feel frozen. The task may feel boring, unclear, too big, uncomfortable, or connected to fear of failure.

This practice helps you stop waiting for the right feeling and create a first step small enough to begin now.

The new chain: notice the freeze → lower the pressure → make the task smaller → start for a few minutes before motivation shows up.

Name the task you are avoiding.

Keep it specific. “Everything” is too big. “Open the email and read it once” is workable.
